About WeatherPro

Your trusted source for real-time weather updates with a modern, interactive interface.

🌐 What is WeatherPro?

WeatherPro is a modern weather tracking application designed to deliver accurate, real-time weather data.

  • 🌦️ Real-time weather updates by city or geolocation
  • 🕒 Hourly and daily forecast charts
  • 🎤 Voice-based search functionality
  • 🖼️ Dynamic weather-based background visuals
  • 📊 Interactive charts with temperature, humidity, wind, and more

🎯 Our Mission

Our goal is to make weather forecasting accessible, visually engaging, and user-friendly for all users—from students to travelers and outdoor planners.
